Before we begin
Arrival
Before we begin, let's take a moment to arrive. Close your eyes if that feels comfortable. Place one hand on your heart. Take a slow breath in through your nose... and release it gently through your nose. Once more — breathing in... and letting everything that doesn't serve you dissolve. Now, from that quiet place — not your mind, but your heart and body — ask yourself: Where am I right now? What do you notice? Heavy or light? Restless or still? Open or closed? Tired or awake? There is no wrong answer. Just describe what is actually here.
